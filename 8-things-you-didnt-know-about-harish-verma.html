➡ Harish Verma Biography

Harish Verma, an Indian actor who works in the Punjabi film industry where he did many films and got much appreciation from the viewers and critics. In 2010, the actor debuted through a film titled ‘Punjaban’ for which he got praised. Later, he then made an appearance in the film named ‘Yaar Annmulle’ through which he gained attention from the larger audience. After gaining success in the field of acting, he then made his singing debut through a song named ‘Ikk Vaari Hor Soch Lae’, which was written by Jaan and produced by B. Praak. However, the star celebrity made his career extensive by working in the acting and singing fields. Let us know more about his personal and professional life by giving a read to these unknown facts we have compiled for you!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Harish Verma

1- Famous for

Harish Verma is the man who has grabbed the attention of the wider audience with his amazing acting chops but he became famous for playing the role of Jatt Tinka in the movie named ‘Yaar Annmulle’ released in 2011.

2- Well-praised Performance

Apart from acting in Punjabi films, Verma also made appearances in Indian television serials and one of the best is ‘Naa Aana Is Des Laado’ in which he portrayed the role of Avtar Singh Sangwan which was aired in 2009.

3- Graduated in Psychology

The actor is not just famous for his acting and singing skills, yet he is so intelligent as well. He holds a bachelors degree in the field of Psychology. Wowww… that’s amazing to know!

4- Avid Reader

Alongside the acting and singing profession, the actor also manages time for reading as he is an avid reader and also loves writing whenever he gets free time, he loves to spend with his books.

5- Enthusiast Traveler

The actor is kind of an adventurous person who loves and enjoys traveling. He loves visiting various places to get some exposure and experience by knowing about people and their culture. His favorite destination place is Paris.

6- Favorite Actors

His favorite actors are Shahrukh Khan, Guggu Gill, Varinder, Alia Bhatt, Sargun Mehta, and Neeru Bajwa whom he admires for their wonderful acting chops and heart-winning performances.

7- Other Favorites

Harish Verma’s most favorite theatre personality is Gursharan Singh who is famous for his admiring performances and work. While his favorite colors are Black and Blue.

8- Love for Acting and Singing

According to media reports, it is said that Harish Verma had a love for acting and singing since his childhood. And following his love and passion, he then pursued acting and singing as a career.
