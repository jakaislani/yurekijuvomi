Badou Jack Net Worth And Career

The Swedish boxer has garnered enormous fame and fortune. Celebrity Net Worth reported that Badou Jack’s net worth is $4 million.

His fortune primarily benefits from his earning as a professional boxer. However, Jack must also have other ventures – endorsement deals – which benefit his riches significantly.

The successful boxer began his amateur career in 2001. He has participated in the light heavyweight and super middleweight divisions.

As an amateur, Jack represented The Gambia at the 2008 Summer Olympics. In June 2009, Jack won his professional boxing debut match against Maxim Nikonorov. He and Marco Antonio Periban fought to a draw in September 2013 for the WBC-NABF super-middleweight championship.

Moreover, Jack won the WBC super-middleweight championship in April 2015, defeating Anthony Dirrell. In January 2017, Badou fought James DeGale to a draw for the vacant The Ring and IBF super-middleweight belts.

The WBC Cruiserweight title holder defeated Nathan Cleverly to win WBA (Regular) light-heavyweight title in August 2017. Jack’s victory over Blake McKernan in November 2020 improved his record to 23-3-3.
