Who can repair Karlach?

Cooling off Karlach is in direct relation to her Companion Quest, The Hellion’s Heart. In order to do so you’ll need to employ the work of a Tiefling blacksmith named Dammon. He’s located in the Emerald Grove in the top corner at X:179, Y:562.

How do you repair Karlach’s heart in Baldur’s Gate 3?

How do you cool down Karlach?

Back in your camp, talk to Karlach and say that you know of a way to cool her off. She’ll simply reply with “I’m waiting” and her dialogue will not change. To fix this, you can either throw water at her, cast a water or ice spell, or shoot an ice arrow.
