 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  473 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As a platform, Twitch has really grown over the past few years. Moreover, streamers like xQc, Pokimane, HasanAbi, and others dominate the show. Furthermore, one particular streamer has been on the top charts for years now. Yes, it’s Kai Cenat. Kai has totally taken over the streaming community with his explosive personality and content. Furthermore, recently Kai Cenat collabed with the famous rapper Nicki Minaj, and now Nicki has wished him and his twin sister on their 22nd birthday.

Streamers always produce content that keeps the fans entertained and grows their fan base. Moreover, many streamers like N3on, Sneako, and others have gained a lot of popularity in the past few months. However, Kai Cenat’s popularity is on a whole other level. The Twitch streamer gained an influx of subscribers after his subathon at the start of the year. Fans totally love the streamer.

All this popularity is due to Kai’s own skills and hilariously engaging content. Furthermore, the streamer has also made a cameo in the Paramount+ movie ‘Good Burger 2’! Also, with the rise in his popularity, Kai has done many high-society collabs, too. And Kai’s collab with Nicki Minaj went really wild. Additionally, his friendship with Nicki Minaj has reached such a level that the rapper wished Kai Cenat and his twin sister, on their 22nd birthday.

Kai Cenat and his twin sister get their 22nd birthday wishes from Rapper Nicki Minaj

This year has been golden for Kai’s fame and popularity. The streamer has involved himself in loads of happenings throughout the year that have made him quite infamous. The latest riot in Union Square Park occurred as a result of Kai’s PS giveaway event going horribly wrong, with the supporters going wild. These errors, nevertheless, pale in comparison to Kai’s originality and commitment to content creation! And his recent collab was pretty explosive, too!

Kai Cenat has previously done many streams with pop artists like Drake, and his recent 7-Days Prison livestream had many pop artists, too. However, this time, Kai Cenat collabed with no other than the famous rapper, Nicki Minaj. Furthermore, the stream went really wild. The duo built a really great relationship, and Nicki Minaj even wished Kai Cenat and his twin sister Kaiya on their 22nd birthday through her Instagram story.

Furthermore, Nicki posted, “Happy Pink Birthday to the one and only @kaicenat” & for Kai’s sister, she posted: “Happy Pink birthday pretty girl!!!! @kaiyacenat”. This really had fans titling since Nicki herself wished Kai that, too, on her official Instagram handle!

This birthday wish shows how well Nicki Minaj has come to know Kai Cenat. Furthermore, the recent livestream together was a really fun one. Kai, along with his whole family, danced with Nicki Minaj on her new song. The entire livestream was great and saw a viewer count of over 300K!
