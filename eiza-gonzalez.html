Is a Mexican actress Eiza Gonzalez dating Timothee Chalamet, an actor known for Call Me by Your Name

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  611 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Eiza Gonzalez is the Mexican entertainment personality. Gonzalez who received Kids Choice Awards got worldwide recognition after starring as Dolores "Lola" Valente in the Mexican melodrama telenovela Lola: Once Upon a Time (Lola, erase una vez).

The actress also known as Eiza Gonzalez Reyna was born on January 30, 1990, Mexico City, Mexico. She is the daughter of former Mexican model Glenda Reyna (mother) and late. Carlos Gonzalez (father). Her father died in an accident when she was just 12-years-old. Eiza has a brother named Yulen Gonzalez Reyna. 

Eiza attended Edron Academy located in Mexico City. Later she joined the acting class at M & M studio and also attended the educational institution Center for Artistic Education (Centro de Educación Artística) in Mexico City.

Rumors Dating Timothee Chalamet

The 30-years-old actress Eiza has not revealed her exact relationship status. However, there is a rumor swirling in the media that she is in a romantic relationship with an American actor Timothee Chalamet.

The rumors regarding Chalamet and Gonzalez's arise when the two were photographed kissing at the swimming pool in Cabo San Lucas. However, the duo has not explained their relationship status. 

Once when she was interviewed regarding her personal life she said, “I don’t think that it’s necessary to share details about my private life.'' She prefers to keep her love life details within herself.

Eiza's Relationship With Liam Hemsworth

Back in 2013, there was a rumor that an actress Eiza was linked with an Australian actor Liam Hemsworth. Just like with Timothee, the rumors regarding Hemsworth & Gonzalez's romantic affair arise when the two were spotted kissing and hanging together in Las Vegas. 

Eiza and Liam were witnessed together the same day he called off his engagement with his former fiancee Miley Cyrus, an American singer, song-writer. It's still unknown whether Eiza & Liam dated or split up after the rumors.

Past Affairs

From 2011 to 2013 for two years an actress Eiza stayed in a romantic relationship with the reality star and businessman Pepe Diaz.

Then she dated an actor D. J. Cotrona from 2014 to 2015. Cotrona and Gonzalez first met one another at the set of the TV series From Dusk till Dawn: The Series. Even after the separation, the twosome is sharing a perfect bond like a good friend. 

Similarly, in 2018 she began dating an actor and fashion model Josh Duhamel. As per US Weekly, Gonzalez and Duhamel first met at a party in Minneapolis, MN. However, this relationship also ended in a break-up after five months in July 2018.
