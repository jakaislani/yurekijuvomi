Learn About Bri Dodd From The Voice Australia 2022: Who Is She?

Bri Dodd is a musician who has recently made headlines for her incredible blind audition performance on Season 11 of The Voice Australia.

Dodd, a 25-year-old Victorian fireman who performs Jess Mauboy’s smash Fallin’, becomes the second performer this season to turn both Jess and Rita’s chairs in the blind audition.

She performs it at a higher pitch than Jess, confessing that she’s taking a chance, but her voice is so incredible that she doesn’t have to be concerned. Both judges are head over heels in love with her.

Similarly, Jess and Rita both turn around and are taken aback to see this young girl when they were plainly anticipating a grizzled old country and western dude.

A Guy comments that the crowd adored Bri, but that the song selection may not have been the best.

25 Years Old Bri Dodd Contestant Has The Voice Australia Judges In Shock

Bri Dodd, a 25-year-old The Voice Australia contestant from Victoria, stunned the judges with her incredible performance in the blind audition.

One of the panelists commented that he always thinks it’s strange when an artist copies a coach’s music but then chooses them as their coach.

This is exactly what happened to Bri, and people can see she was upset about it because she was a little teary when the voice contestant will go with Rita.

Rita, like everyone else, appeared to be completely taken aback by Bri’s effort. Her performance has piqued the interest of the public.
