Grown-ish (2018-2021)

Francia Raisa’s talent and versatility as an actress are evident in her role in the TV series “Grown-ish.” From 2018 to 2021, Raisa portrayed the character Ana Torres, a determined and ambitious college student. As part of the main cast, Raisa’s performance captivated audiences and showcased her ability to bring depth and authenticity to her characters.

In “Grown-ish,” Raisa’s character navigates through the challenges and experiences of young adulthood, addressing important social and cultural issues along the way. The show, created by Kenya Barris, is a spinoff of the critically acclaimed series “Black-ish” and has become popular among viewers of all ages.

Raisa’s involvement in “Grown-ish” not only solidified her standing as a talented actress but also allowed her to connect with a diverse audience. Through her compelling portrayal of Ana Torres, Raisa demonstrated her ability to bring complex and relatable characters to life.

It is worth mentioning that “Grown-ish” received positive reviews for its realistic depiction of college life and its exploration of relevant social issues. Raisa’s contribution to the show undoubtedly added depth and authenticity to the narrative, further cementing her reputation as an accomplished actress.

How I Met Your Father (2022-2023)

Francia Raisa is currently involved in the highly-anticipated spinoff series, “How I Met Your Father,” which premiered in 2022. She plays the character Valentina in the show, adding another impressive project to her growing list of TV series appearances.

After a successful run on “Grown-ish,” Raisa continues to captivate audiences with her talent and versatility in “How I Met Your Father.” The series, a spinoff of the popular sitcom “How I Met Your Mother,” follows a group of friends navigating love and relationships in New York City.
